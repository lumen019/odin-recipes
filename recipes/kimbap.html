Classic Kimbap

Description

Kimbap is Koreaâ€™s most popular on-the-go meal! Learn how to make a classic version at home with this recipe with a step-by-step guide!

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2-3 cups cooked short-grain white rice
  • Seasoning: 1 Tbsp sesame oil, 1/2 tsp salt
  • Seaweed: 4 sheets of dried seaweed
  • Fillings (Prep all beforehand):
    • Spinach: 1 bunch, blanched and seasoned
    • Carrots: 1 medium, julienned and lightly stir-fried
    • Eggs: 2-3, beaten, cooked as a thin omelet, and sliced into strips
    • Pickled Radish: 4 strips of yellow pickled radish (danmuji)
    • Protein (Optional): Cooked beef, ham, or imitation crab sticks, sliced into strips

Steps

  1. Season the warm rice with sesame oil and salt, then let it cool slightly.
  2. Set up a bamboo rolling mat and place a sheet of seaweed on it, shiny side down.
  3. Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of rice over two-thirds of the seaweed.
  4. Arrange the fillings in a line across the center of the rice.
  5. Roll the seaweed tightly over the fillings, using the mat to keep the shape firm.
  6. Wet the top edge to seal the roll and gently press it in the mat to compact it.
  7. Brush the roll with sesame oil and slice it into bite-size pieces with a sharp knife.
